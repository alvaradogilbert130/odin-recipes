Matcha Latte

A Matcha Latte is a creamy, green-colored tea beverage made by whisking finely ground green tea powder (matcha) into hot or cold milk. It's a popular alternative to regular coffee, offering a smooth, frothy texture and a distinctive, vegetal taste. Many coffee shops and tearooms serve both hot and iced Matcha Lattes. The matcha powder is often sweetened, and the amount of milk used can be adjusted to taste.

Ingredents

  • ½ to 1 teaspoon matcha powder
  • ¼ cup hot water
  • ¼ cup coconut milk, warmed
  • Maple syrup, honey, stevia, or sweetener of choice, optional

Steps

  1. Sift your matcha so it is lump-free.
  2. Spoon the matcha into a large mug. Add the hot water and whisk briskly, in an up and down motion, until frothy - about 30 seconds or so. Add the coconut milk and whisk until well combined.
  3. Taste and add additional water, coconut milk, and/or sweetener to your liking.
